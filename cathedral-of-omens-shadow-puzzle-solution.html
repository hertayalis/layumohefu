    Here's how to solve the Cathedral of Omens shadow puzzle in Remnant 2.

    First Published July 21, 2023, 23:04

    Remnant 2 is a brutal and challenging game, not just in combat but also when it comes to its numerous environmental puzzles. You will encounter at least one in every realm, and solving them can get you some unique gear and valuable crafting resources. 

    The Cathedral of Omens shadow puzzle in the Yaesha region is an optional puzzle. The trick is to align the shadows in the right way to open the doors. However, it's easy to get confused and overwhelmed because there's so much to keep track of. As such, here's our guide, which explains how to solve the Cathedral of Omens shadow puzzle in Remnant 2.

    Table of Contents

    How to Solve the Cathedral of Omens Shadow Puzzle in Remnant 2

    As soon as you enter, you will notice six different symbols on the ground. These denote The Sun, The Moon, Life, Death, The Doe, and The Ravager. These symbols' shadows can be rotated with levers in this room. There are three levers, and each moves the shadows in unique ways. You have to tweak all three in the correct manner to open the doors.

    There are two locked doors here, and both require different shadow arrangements on the ground. You can find a notebook on the first floor that provides hints to open these doors. However, it's still pretty cryptic, and if you don't want to waste your time-solving riddles, below we have provided the correct patterns to open both doors. 

    Door 1 puzzle solution (Calamity)

    It's the door on the opposite side of the room from where you first enter. You can notice a left-side-tilted triangle pattern above the door. It's the symbol you have to replicate with the shadows to open the door. Have a look at the screenshot above to see which symbol goes where. 

    Once you have unlocked the door, head inside to get a Faith Seed. 

    Door 2 puzzle solution (The Blood Moon)

    It's the door on the right-hand side of the room from where you first enter. You can notice a slightly left-side-tilted triangle pattern above the door. Again, you have to replicate that pattern with the shadows on the ground to open the door. The screenshot above should give you an idea of where each symbol goes. 

    Once you have unlocked the door, head inside and you will find two chests. Open them up to get random crafting materials and Relic fragments. 

    That concludes our guide on how to solve the Cathedral of Omens shadow puzzle in Remnant 2.
