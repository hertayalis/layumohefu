    Gigi and Bella Hadid Looked So Glamorous at a New York City Gala

    Individually, Gigi and Bella Hadid are style stars in their own right, often dominating headlines for leading the street style set in New York City. But put them together, and it's a fashion supernova.

    02 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Individually, Gigi and Bella Hadid are style stars in their own right, often dominating headlines for leading the street style set in New York City. But put them together, and it's a fashion supernova.

    Last night, the model powerhouses and sisters made a joint appearance at the 2022 Prince's Trust Gala, which Prince Charles founded in 1976 to target youth unemployment, held in New York City. Dressed to the nines in their own monochrome looks, they wore bold shades from opposite ends of the color spectrum.

    Gigi looked like Haute Couture Barbie in an entirely hot pink outfit, composed of pieces fresh off Valentino's fall/winter 2022 runway. She wore a floral jacquard minidress with off-the-shoulder sleeves over leggings and the house's enviable pair of pointed platform pumps. She also carried a matching croc-embossed handbag.

    Getty Images

    Getty Images

    Meanwhile, Bella channeled the glamour of Old Hollywood in a stunning vintage Dior number. Her strapless black ball gown included floral detailing on the bodice, and she paired the dress with sheer opera gloves and black Louboutin pumps. Jewels were also a core component of her look, with the Kin Euphorics partner wearing dazzling diamonds in the form of a necklace, bracelet, and earrings.

    Getty Images

    Getty Images

    In the past, the sisters have occasionally ventured out to make stylish duo appearances on the red carpet. They memorably coordinated in sultry neutrals during the 2019 MTV Video Music Awards. Then, Bella wore a midriff-baring ruched minidress, while Gigi wore a mesh boned corset by Tom Ford paired with sheeny tapered trousers.
