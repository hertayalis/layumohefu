    Apink Debut

    Apink made its debut in 2011 which is precisely on April 19. By then they had recorded their first EP entitled Seven Springs of Apink.

    Who left in Apink?

    Is Apink still together?

    Despite the change of agency, the company assured fans that Apink would remain a six-member group.
