      Fans ofare currently enjoying the fifth and final season on Netflix, which sees Jane once again torn between the father of her child, Rafael, and her back-from-the-dead-amnesiac husband, Michael. Since the fifth season will be the show's last, the executive producer Jennie Urman has confirmed that viewers will finally find out the identity of the narrator, who cheekily comments on the events surrounding the characters in every episode. She added that there have been "hints throughout" regarding his true identity. As such, one popular theory is that the narrator is actually Jane and Rafael's son Mateo, but all grown up, and all of the signs seem to fit perfectly!

      Is little Mateo the real narrator?
