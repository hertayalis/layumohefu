    Lil Uzi Vert said they are planning on removing all their tattoos.

    During a recent conversation with Ryan Leuteritz for L.A. Weekly, the Philly rapper was asked which tattoo was their favorite. They responded, “my favorite tattoo [is] none of them. I’m trying to get them removed. All of them. I wanna go corporate.”

    Check out a clip from the interview below.

    During an interview with The FADER back in 2017, Uzi revealed they got their first face tattoo when they were homeless after their mom kicked them out of the house for quitting their job at Bottom Dollar Food. “I had a job for f**king four days. I couldn’t do it. I’m not normal. I was in there just thinking, Why am I doing this?” they said. “It was like, ‘If I get this face tattoo, I got to focus. I can’t go in nobody’s office with a suit on with this shit on my face. I got to focus on what I want to do.”
