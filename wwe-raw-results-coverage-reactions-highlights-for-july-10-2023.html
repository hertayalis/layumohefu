    27 Jun 2024, 00:00

    It’s Monday night, and you know what that means! WWE RAW will go down tonight, and Ringside News has got you covered with live play-by-play results coverage.

    WWE RAW will start this week at 8:00 PM EST, as always. match-by-match highlights and results from the show will take place on this very page. Feel free to leave any reactions in the comment section below.

    Tonight promises another exciting night of action as Seth Rollins faces off against Dominik Mysterio. We’ll also get to hear what Cody Rhodes has to say when he delivers a special message for Brock Lesnar. The updated match card is as follows:

    • Logan and Ricochet come face-to-face
    • Cody Rhode delivers a special message to Brock Lesnar
    • No Disqualification Match: Tommaso Ciampa vs. The Miz
    • Drew McIntyre & Matt Riddle vs. Ludwig Kaiser & Giovanni Vinci
    • Maxxine Dupri graduation ceremony
    • Seth Rollins vs. Dominik Mysterio

    WWE RAW Opener:

    The opening package for the show airs.

    Finn Balor’s music plays and he walks out to the ring. Balor gets on the mic and says the way things went down at MITB doesn’t sit right with him. He says the issues between himself and Seth haven’t been put to rest because of Damian Priest.

    Rhea Ripley’s music hit and she comes out with Dominik. Rhea says they can talk about whatever Finn has to say backstage. Balor says Priest cost him the World Heavyweight Championship match at MITB. Rhea stops Balor from talking and they argue in the ring. Balor leaves the ring and walks away.

    Rhea says what happened was their business and they still run RAW. She dares anyone to come out and question that fact because she is still the most dominant woman in WWE. She also says that Dominik will beat Seth Rollins tonight.

    Seth Rollins’ music plays and he comes out. He tells Dominik to shut up. He says he came here to watch the Judgment Day implode. Dominik says the only thing imploding will be Seth’s head. Rollins laughs and says he is going to whip Dominik’s ass tonight.

    Drew McIntyre & Matt Riddle vs. Ludwig Kaiser & Giovanni Vinci

    Drew McIntyre’s theme song plays and he comes out to the ring. Riddle’s music plays next and he walks to the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Damian Priest is backstage and Rhea and Dominik enter. Rhea tells him that they need to start acting like men and squash it. Priest says he will speak to him again.

    Imperium’s music plays and they make their way to the ring.

    The match starts with Riddle and Kaiser. Riddle takes down Kaiser. Kaiser takes down Riddle. Kaiser attacks Riddle in the corner. Riddle comes back with a gutwrench suplex. Drew tags in and Kaiser tags out.

    McIntyre knocks down Vinci. He tags out and Riddle attacks Vinci. Vinci tags out. Kaiser dropkicks Riddle sending him outside the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Vinci with a big boot to Riddle. He tags out. Cover! 1…2..kick out. Kaiser goes to work on Riddle. Kaiser with an uppercut. He then drives Riddle to the mat. Riddle with the overhead kick to Kaiser. Vinci tags in and hits a suplex. Cover! 1….2….kick out.

    Kaiser tags in. Riddle with a knee to Kaiser. He tags out and Drew attacks Imperium. He hits a belly-to-belly suplex to Vinci. He follows it with a neckbreaker. Drew powerbombs Vinci.

    Gunther drags Drew out of the ring. Riddle takes him out. Drew hits a Claymore on Vinci for the win.

    Winner: Drew McIntyre & Matt Riddle

    Gunther and Kaiser don’t seem impressed with Giovanni Vinci.

    Judgment Day is backstage. Balor says if it wasn’t for Priest he would be World Champion. Rhea asks them to stop acting like children. Priest says it wasn’t his intention to cost him the match at MITB. He says they can go back to the way things were. Balor says they can clean the slate but he has issues with Seth so he gets him first. Priest agrees and says he knows Balor can beat Rollins.

    *Commercial break*

    Kevin and Sami are interviewed backstage. Sami says he doesn’t know what’s next and they going to continue beating everyone who steps up to them. Rhea Ripley and Dominik show up. Rhea says they didn’t say the Judgment Day’s name. She says they could take the tag titles if they wanted to. She then says that Dominik is going to smash Seth Rollins’ face over and over again. They walk away.

    Dominik Mysterio vs. Seth Rollins

    Dominik Mysterio’s music plays and he walks to the ring with Rhea Ripley.

    *Commercial break*

    Seth Rollins’ music plays and he comes out to the ring. Dominik attacks Rollins who fights back. Balor and Priest attack Rollins from behind. Kevin Owens’s music hit and they come out to rescue Rollins.

    Rollins gets a steel chair and attacks Dominik as the rest of Judgment Day escapes the ring.

    Winner: No Contest

    Logan Paul is shown walking backstage.

    *Commercial break*

    Ricochet’s music plays and he walks to the ring. He gets on the mic and says every risk that he takes is for the fans. He then says tonight he is here for Logan Paul. He says he knows Logan is here and says he has accomplished a lot in the WWE and they have crossed paths on many occasions and one of those occasions was when Logan attacked him from behind after losing at MITB.

    Ricochet says he didn’t appreciate Logan talking about him on his podcast and he calls him out. Logan Paul’s music hit and he comes out. Logan insults Ricochet. He says Ricochet is a human highlight reel and one of the most spectacular superstars he has seen but he stands by what he said on his podcast because everyone in the MITB match was more interested in preventing Paul from winning.

    Ricochet says none of these people wanted him near the briefcase. He says he wouldn’t be standing here if he was unprofessional. Ricochet he was going to come out here and challenge him to a match but now all he wants to do his hurt him but before he does, he warns Logan not to run his mouth about Ricochet again.

    Logan says he won’t stand here and be intimidated by Ricochet. He says Ricochet wants a match against so that he can go viral. He says this isn’t going to work out between them. Ricochet dives out of the ring. Logan says that was cool but this isn’t going to work out. Paul tries to sucker punch Ricochet but he reverses and hits a slice bread #2.

    Becky Lynch vs. Zoey Stark

    Becky Lynch’s music hit and she walks out to the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Zoey Stark’s music hit and she walks out with Trish Stratus.

    The match begins and Stark knocks down Lynch. Stratus with a dropkick to Stark. She performs multiple arm drags and goes to work on Stark. Trish trips Lynch and Stark attacks her in the corner.

    Stark hits a springboard dropkick sending Lynch out of the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Lynch attacks Stark in the corner and sends her out of the ring. Lynch with a baseball dropkick. Lynch sends Stark into the barricade multiple times. She sends her back into the ring. Lynch with a pumphandle slam. Cover! 1…2…kick out.

    Becky climbs the top rope and goes for a dive but Stark evades and hits a clothesline. Stark springboards onto Lynch. Cover! 1…2..kick out. She goes for Z360 but Lynch counters and hits the reverse DDT. Cover! 1…2…kick out.

    Becky goes for the disarm her but Stark escapes and kicks her in the face. Stark rolls up Lynch but the referee sees her holding the rope. Zoey goes for Z360 but Lynch escapes and takes out Trish. Zoey rolls her up for the win.

    Winner: Zoey Stark

    Owens, Sami, and Rollins are backstage. Rollins says they need to come up with a plan. Sami says they have been dealing with Judgment Day for some time but they can’t beat them. Sami says they are the three champions in the company. Owens says they go out there and destroy Judgment Day tonight.

    *Commercial break*

    Otis and Chad Gable are in the ring. Gable says they are here to honor the achievement of the first-ever female graduate of the Alpha Academy. He says she helped them defeat the Viking Raiders and hence, it is his pleasure to present the Alpha Academy diploma to Maxxine Dupri.

    Maxxine’s music plays and she walks to the ring. Maxxine says she first thought that Gable was a disgusting troll but he is a good mentor and teacher. She says thanks to Gable’s training she was able to win her first match against Valhalla. She also thanks Otis. She then thanks the WWE Universe. She says they are just getting started.

    Gable then says they have a tradition. Otis and Gable wear their varsity jackets. He asks Maxxine to take her spot in the center of the ring and take off her cap. He also asks her to take off her gown and put on her own jacket.

    The Viking Raiders’ music hit and they come out to the ring. Valhalla attacks Maxxine from behind. She then takes her jacket and leaves the ring.

    Shayna Baszler is warming up backstage.

    *Commercial break*

    Shayna Baszler vs. Emma

    Shayna Baszler’s music plays and she walks to the ring.

    The match begins and attacks Emma in the corner. Emma comes back with a dropkick and a splash in the corner. Shayna recovers and hits a clothesline. Shayna stomps the arm of Emma. She then locks in a sleeper hold and Emma taps out.

    Winner: Shayna Baszler

    Ronda Rousey’s music hit and she comes out to the ring. Ronday attacks Shayna who locks in the sleeper hold. Rousey escapes and goes for an armbar as Shayna escapes the ring.

    Cody Rhodes is shown walking backstage.

    *Commercial break*

    Cody Rhodes’ theme song plays and he walks to the ring. He gets on the mic and says it feels like the crowd is in the ring with him. He says the reason it feels like that is because what he has been telling the fans since he returned is real.

    He admits his momentum in the ring is shaky because of a mountain named Brock Lesnar. He says Lesnar is not here tonight. He also says Brock will be here next week. He then says everybody has a Brock Lesnar.

    He then says he dreams of a rubber match against Brock Lesnar at SummerSlam. Cody then says he is going to finish the chapter that Lesnar started. He says he must have another match against Lesnar because then he will be next in line.

    No Disqualification Match: Tommaso Ciampa vs. The Miz

    The Miz’s music plays and he comes out to the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Tommaso Ciampa’s theme song plays and he walks to the ring. Ciampa grabs a steel chair from the ringside. He drags The Miz outside and sends him into the barricade. Ciampa sends him into the ring and attacks him.

    Miz rolls out of the ring. Ciampa sends Miz over the announcer’s desk. He hits him with a jumping knee. Ciampa sends Miz into the ring. Miz recovers and hits a big boot. The Miz attacks Ciampa in the corner. He attacks Ciampa in the corner. Miz goes for a dive but Ciampa hits him with a steel chair.

    Ciampa wraps the chair around Miz’s head and sends him into the turnbuckle.

    *Commercial break*

    The Miz sends Ciampa into the ring. Ciampa hits him with a jumping knee. He hits a back suplex on the outside. Ciampa launches Miz off the announcer’s desk onto the barricade. He gets a couple of weapons from under the ring.

    Miz kicks Ciampa. Miz attacks Ciampa in the corner. Miz tries to hit Ciampa with a trash can but Ciampa catches him with a knee. He attacks him in the corner with several running knees. Ciampa sets up a table in the ring. Ciampa plants Miz through the table. Cover! 1….2….Bronson Reed drags Ciampa out of the ring and attacks him. He sends him back into the ring and hits the tsunami. The Miz pins Ciampa.

    Winner: Tommaso Ciampa

    Liv and Raquel are talking backstage but they are interrupted by Green and Devile who ask them who gave them permission to show up for their match. Raquel says they are ready to defend the titles. Rhea walks in and warns Raquel to stay out of her business. Raquel asks her to deal with it.

    Raquel Rodriguez’s music plays and she comes out with Liv Morgan.

    Sonya Deville & Chelsea Green vs. Kayden Carter & Katana Chance

    Sonya Deville’s music plays and she comes out to the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Katana Chance and Kayden Carter’s music plays and they walk to the ring.

    The match starts with Sonya and Kayden. Kayden takes down Sonya and tags out. Katana attacks her and tags out. Chelsea tags in and attacks Kayden. Green slams Carter into the second turnbuckle.

    Green sets her up on the top rope. Chance tags in and hits a splash off the top rope. Cover! 1…2…Sonya makes the save. Chelsea rolls up Chance and gets the win with leverage from Sonya Deville.

    Winner: Chelsea Green vs. Sonya Deville

    McIntyre and Riddle are interviewed by Byron Saxton. Drew says it felt good to beat Imperium. He says he is sick of all these groups. Riddle says he is sick and tired of Imperium and he says he will beat Gunther next week. Drew asks him to hold off on the match till the next week but Riddle says he got Giovanni Vinci and Ludwig Kaiser banned from ringside. Drew seems happy with that and they leave for drinks.

    Seth Rollins, Kevin Owens, & Sami Zayn vs. Judgment Day

    Seth Rollins’ music plays and he walks to the ring.

    *Commercial break*

    Kevin Owens’ music plays and he comes out. Sami Zayn’s music plays next and he joins him and they walk to the ring. The Judgment Day’s theme song plays and they come down to the ring.

    The match starts with Seth Rollins and Dominik. Dominik runs away from Rollins. Balor tags in. Rollins attacks him in the corner. Balor tags out. Priest attacks Rollins. He attacks him in the corner. Rollins comes back and slams Priest into the turnbuckle. Owens tags in and he continues to attack Priest. Rollins and Sami attack Balor and Dominik and a brawl breaks out.

    Owens wipes out the Judgment Day.

    *Commercial break*

    Priest goes to work on Owens. Priest with a corner dive. He then hits a leg drop. Cover! 1….2…kick out. Balor tags in and continues the assault on Owens. Owens comes back with a DDT. Dominik tags in and Rollins also tags in. He attacks Dominik and hits a clothesline.

    Rollins sends Priest out of the ring. Rollins superkicks Dominik. Rollins goes for the powerbomb but Dominik gauges the eyes of Rollins. He tags out. Priest hits a clothesline. Cover! 1….2…kick out.

    Rollins superkicks Priest and knocks down Balor. Cover! 1…2…Balor makes the save. Balor tags in. Rollins with a rolling elbow. Dominik tags in. Balor hits the reverse DDT and Dominik hit the frog splash. Cover! 1…2..kick out. Balor hit the sling blade. Rollins comes back with a superkick.

    Rollins tags out. Sami hits the crossbody and a blue thunder bomb. Balor recovers and hits the sling blade. Balor hits the shotgun dropkick. He goes for coup de grace but misses. Sami hits the blue thunder bomb. Cover! 1…2…Dominik makes the save. Sami hits the exploder suplex in the corner. Rhea distracts Sami and Priest hit the South of Heaven and Balor hit the coup de grace for the win.

    Winner: Judgment Day

    This ends our live coverage of WWE RAW!
