    Delve into the glitz and glamour that followed the Academy Awards  with our coverage of the afterparties...

      Following the glamorous 2024 Oscars ceremony at the Dolby Theatre, a constellation of stars gathered to honour the evening's victors at the Vanity Fair afterparty as well as Elton John's legendary Annual Academy Awards Viewing Party (now in it's 32nd year). This former soirée is being expertly hosted by Radhika Jones, and these gatherings have evolved into an equally anticipated showcase of fashion, offering yet another dazzling spectacle to the formal proceedings of the night.

      The red carpet may have displayed a parade of breathtaking gowns, but the afterparty attire worn by style icons like Rosie Huntington-Whiteley and Kendall Jenner elevated the fashion stakes even higher. Their ensembles, featuring mesmerising sequins and silhouettes, underscore the event's legacy as a stage for sartorial excellence. 

      Scroll on to witness the most glamorous after party gowns of the night…

      Emily Ratajkowski

      Emily Ratajkowski wore a sleek white Jacquemus gown with a bold architectural detail. The sculptural overlay, created a striking visual impact alongside her Messika jewellery.

      Da'Vine Joy Randolph

      Academy Award winner Da'Vine Joy Randolph stunned in a black gown with a sheer bodice detailed with embroidery, paired with matching gloves. Although her diamond cuff bracelet was absolutely dazzling, the best accessory was her gleaming golden Oscar.

      Pamela Anderson

      Pamela served 70s glamour in floor-length dress from Oscar de la Renta's FW24 RTW collection. The fabric was adorned with vibrant yellow floral patterns, embellished with sequins and beading.

      Kylie Jenner

      Kendall kept true to her new 'quiet luxury' aesthetic which was light-years away from her previous Oscars 'fits. With pared-back makeup and a classic maroon dress from Ludovic de Saint Sernin's FW24 RTW collection, she re-invented her approach to the red carpet.

      Kim Kardashian

      Kim Kardashian served a fresh interpretation of Hollywood glamour, with a Balenciaga gown featuring a classic mermaid shape juxtaposed with dramatic sculptural point detail at the neckline that added an element of stark modernity. 

      Adwoa Aboah

      Adwoa Aboah stunned in a Marni FW24 RTW halter-neck leather dress which flared out into a wide, A-line skirt. Red heels added a surprising pop of colour to the futuristic ensemble.

      Rosie Huntington-Whiteley

      Rosie Huntington-Whiteley plumped for a sophisticated black velvet gown with a high neck and long, flowing chiffon cape that extends from the shoulders, creating a dramatic effect. Paired with opera-length leather gloves, the look evoked contemporary sci-fi elegance that wouldn't look out of place in the Dune film series.

      Kendall Jenner

      The supermodel donned a Maison Margiela sheer lace black dress in a mesmerising silhouette and paired it with Christian Louboutin heels. With subtle added sparkle and detailing around the waist, Kendall was every inch the modern style icon.

      Anya Taylor-Joy

      Anya Taylor-Joy defied convention in a custom-made Miss Sohee black mini dress with a plunging neckline, detailed with a lace and sequinned bustier. The dress was accentuated by its elaborate silver shoulder embellishments and a matching bejewelled headpiece, offset by striking pearls and black Christian Louboutin heels.

      Margot Robbie

      Margot Robbie ditched her Barbie-inspired wardrobe for a moment to don a striking Mugler SS96 RTW metallic gold corset dress with a structured bodice and draped golden shawl.

      Emma Stone

      Proudly clutching her Oscar, Emma donned a shimmering, silver-toned Louis Vuitton dress with a semi-sheer overlay richly adorned with metallic mirror accents that created a dazzling, reflective effect. The asymmetrical hem added a modern touch.

      Greta Lee

      Greta Lee wore an exquisite champagne-hued Loewe satin gown with beaded detailing along the neckline and bias cut. The vintage silhouette and subtle accents spoke to timeless elegance.

      Lupita Nyong'o

      Lupita Nyong'o always pushes the boundaries while maintaining her trademark red carpet elegance. Her black dress featured a dazzling sequinned gradient from green to black, creating an artistic and modern look.

      Laura Harrier

      Laura Harrier radiated old-Hollywood glamour in a classic, form-fitting golden gown adorned with sequins.

      Lily Aldridge

      Lily Aldridge radiated elegance in a sophisticated black gown with a unique feathered neckline.

      Florence Pugh

      Florence Pugh proved sheer could still exuded innocence in a unique translucent dress with white feather applications and sheer skirt from Simone Rocha's recent collection for Jean Paul Gaultier.  Accessorised with a statement 'High Jewellery' necklace from Bulgari which she paired with the brand's earrings, and embellished Christian Louboutin heels, she proved she's the undisputed champion of red carpet dressing. 

      Charlotte Tilbury

      Charlotte Tilbury served boho chic in a fitted gown adorned with vibrant red roses and golden accents that exuded a baroque aesthetic. The gown's fringe details added movement as the MUA swayed down the red carpet.

      A.V. Rockwell

      A.V. Rockwell stunned in an elegant blush pink gown boasting a unique off-the-shoulder neckline. The dress, which ends with a simple hem, allowed the dramatic bell-shaped sleeves to stand out.

      Sofía Vergara

      The star went with a classic black mermaid silhouette number. The strapless gown's cut-out feature are accented by large black bows, to create a feminine look.

      Chrissy Teigen

      Chrissy Teigen donned a sheer shimmering silver gown embellished with floral appliqués, that featured a high slit. She completed the look with metallic heels.

      Kerry Washington

      Kerry Washington wore a glamorous floor-length black gown with intricate detailing from Giambattista Valli's SS24 Couture collection. It featured a semi-sheer bodice embellished with crystals and a ruffled off-the-shoulder neckline. She tied her glam look together with a pair of black satin platform heels from Christian Louboutin and Messika jewellery.

      Chloë Sevigny

      Chloë Sevigny dressed in a bold red one-shoulder mini dress which was proved good things come in small packages. The Simone Rocha SS24 RTW dress came complete with an oversized, dramatic bow draped over the shoulder and cascaded down the front.

      Katharine McPhee

      The singer wore a beaded, sheer Valdrian Sahiti gown with adorable black bow straps. Adorned with intricate beading and sequin detailing, the dress proved more is more. 

      Radhika Jones

      Radhika Jones stunned in a royal blue gown with a sleek silhouette, enhanced with a sweeping over-the-shoulder adornment.

      Leslie Mann

      Leslie Mann wore a two-tone dress featuring a structured velvet bodice and a crisp white knee-length skirt. The look was complemented with a statement necklace and classic black pointed heels.

      Julia Fox

      Julia Fox kept true to her avant-garde style by wearing a bold black mini dress with 'barely there' sheer panels. Her makeup was just as dramatic with smoky eyes and long, wavy blonde hair.

      Elizabeth Hurley & Heidi Klum

      To attend her close pal's bash, Liz Hurley opted for a long-sleeved golden sequined dress with a plunging neckline and a cinched waist from Zuhair Murad. Meanwhile, supermodel Heidi Klum went with a dramatic off-the-shoulder gown featuring crystalline embellished fabric from Valdrian Sahiti.

      Jessica Alba

      Jessica Alba donned a form-fitting, shimmering silver dress from Tamara Ralph featuring an off-shoulder neckline with a subtle sweetheart cut,  adorned with ornate floral appliqué detailing.

      Jennifer Coolidge

      Jennifer Coolidge wore a distinctive piece by Dolce & Gabbana with a striking net overlay of geometric, diamond-shaped cutouts, which added a modern edge to the garment and provided a daring peekaboo effect.

      Sandra Oh

      Sandra Oh wore a captivating celestial gown that featured a gradient ombré design with a dynamic blend of textures. The bodice's deep navy blue hue, embellished with tiny sparkling sequins gradually which become denser and transition into metallic paillettes in a mix of gold, copper and bronze shades.
