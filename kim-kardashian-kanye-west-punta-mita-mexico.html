    Shortly after their fairytale wedding in Italy, Kim Kardashian and Kanye West jetted off to Punta Mita in Mexico for a luxury private holiday – and the glitzy power couple enjoyed their stay so much that they’ve headed there just two months later.Kim and Kanye are thought to be lapping up the luxuries of Casa Aramara, their friend Joe Francis' luxurious beachfront resort.

    Rapper Kanye is keeping busy as he uses the idyllic setting – the only five-star private residence in Punta Mita – to work on his upcoming album, while Kim has been making use of the offerings at the lavish retreat. Mrs Kardashian-West has been soaking up the breathtaking vistas at the luxury resort, and even made a splash in the ocean with a go at jet skiing.

    The reality TV star shared a snap of herself behind the jet ski wheel, accompanied by a friend. "Not easy taking a selfie jet skiing," wrote the 33-year-old, who had tied her wet locks back into a high bun and has her eyes closed against the sea spray. North's mum also made sure she made the most of the sun, soaking up the rays as she enjoyed a siesta. "Let me sleep!!!!" she captioned a photograph of herself lying back in a bikini on a sunlounger.

    At the end of the relaxing day at Casa Aramara, which is nestled amid acres of lush tropical gardens along the beach, the pair enjoyed some downtime together. Kim shared a snap of the duo with her fans, showing herself and her hubby against a beautiful nighttime backdrop of a lit-up pool and the beach in the distance. "#MexicoNightsWithMySexyMan," tweeted Kim.

    Casa Aramara is a long-time favourite of the couple, as well as the rest of the Kardashian-Jenners. The likes of Ashton Kutcher, Courtney Cox, Demi Moore, Eva Longoria and Jennifer Aniston have also been drawn to the luxurious resort, which is just over two hours by air from Los Angeles.
