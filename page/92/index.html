What is Ryuichi Sakamoto famous for? – Ryuichi Sakamoto is a Japanese musician, composer, producer, and actor born on January 17, 1952, in Tokyo, Japan. He is known for his work as a composer in film, television, and video games, as well as for his solo music career and collaborations with other musicians. Sakamoto began his musical career in the early 1970s as a member of the electronic music group Yellow Magic Orchestra (YMO), which was one of the first groups to popularize synth-pop music.
